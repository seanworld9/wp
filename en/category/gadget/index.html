Measures to be taken when a computer is disconnected at the end when connected to a TV

When I put my PC on TV via HDMI, the edges are cut off. When I try to watch a certain Nico Nico Douga, you can see what I see. Well, if I just watch the video, it's not a problem, but the taskbar and application bar are half gone, so it's tough to work or something. I want to do something about it. I was able to change it by using the Intel Graphics advanced settings on the PC side. Open Graphics Properties, select the desired display (TV) and choose Customize Aspect Ratio. As a result, the entire screen is now displayed without being cut off.
